Reglex

Verifiable answers from statutes, rules, and the regulatory record.

The Problem
State agency staff lose hours to fragmented regulatory sources. Statutes, rules, fees, and authority sit across dozens of official sites, creating inconsistent compliance and making cross-state analysis impractical.
The Solution
Reglex consolidates those sources into a citation-grounded research assistant. Staff query in plain English, get verified answers with inline source links, and compare regulations across states in seconds.

i.How It Works

Pipeline · ingest to answer
01 · Sources
Agency Sites

03 · Index

04 · Reason
Agent Router

05 · Answer
Cited Reply
Reflection verifies every citation before delivery.
